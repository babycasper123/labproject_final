Rules for a non-profit organization to be selected:

To be selected the project of the non-profit organization needs to:

1. Use technology and innovative solutions to help foster independence

2. Respect our rules of Ethic : no discrimination of any kind, no promotion of a political or religious organization

3. Have a local, concrete and measurable impact.

Then submitting a new non-profit organization to the Amaris Foundation is simple: collect all the information required with the non-profit organisation then complete the form here. For more details and information, take a look at the FAQ and at the Project Proposal Guidelines Any questions? Any doubts? Don't hesitate to contact us at foundation@amaris.com.
